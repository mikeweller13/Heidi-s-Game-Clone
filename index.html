Heidi's French Toast Recipe

Heidi's french toast is delicious! Trust me, you want to guess correctly!

  • Serves: 4
  • Prep time: 5 minutes
  • Cook time: 15-20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 cup milk
  • 4 slices of bread
  • syrup
  • butter
  • love (optional)

Instructions

  1. Beat egg, vanilla and cinnamon in a bowl.
  2. Pour in milk.
  3. Dip one slice of bread in mixture, covering both sides.
  4. Cook in a skillet on medium heat until both sides are golden brown, about 3 to 4 minutes.
  5. Repeat for remaining 3 slices of bread.
  6. Top with butter and syrup.
  7. Enjoy!

Protip: If you're feeling adventurous, make this the base of a Monte Cristo sandwich!

Tips for making the best french toast

  • Use stale bread. It will soak up more of the mixture.
  • The ideal thickness for a slice of French toast is 3/4" to 1" thick.
  • Mix the custard thoroughly! You don't want pieces of egg white stuck to your toast.
  • Preheat the pan. The custard will seep out and form a "foot" if you don't.
